About Digital Farm Portal

The Digital Farm Portal is a smart solution designed to improve the safety, traceability, and compliance of livestock farms. It helps farmers, veterinarians, and regulators monitor antimicrobial use and ensure safe milk and meat production.

🎯 Mission

Our mission is to protect consumer health by ensuring antimicrobial usage is properly recorded, monitored, and withdrawal periods are strictly followed.

⚡ Key Features

  • 📝 Record Treatments and Dosages
  • 🧪 Track Residue Test Results
  • ⚠ Automatic Withdrawal Alerts
  • 📜 Full Audit Trail for Transparency
  • 📊 Analytics & Reports for Compliance

👥 Project Info

This portal is developed as a prototype project for demonstrating how digital technology can enhance food safety and farm management.
